# Planet Project

## How does it work
When pressing "1", "2" or "3", each one of rockets will burst out of the planet, each one with a different force.
The yellow one has 8 of force, the orange one has 12 of force and the red one has 18 of foce.
You can also use the mousewheel to move the camera forward or backwards.
## What's going on
The planet moves with a constant speed to the right.
The rockets are attracted by its gravity to it.
The rockers leave a trail defining where they have passed by.
